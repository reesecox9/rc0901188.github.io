Interests

Rugs

In my spare time I love to make rugs. I do this through a process called tufting. I use a projector to cast my image to my frame and trace the design. Then I use my tufting gun to add the yarn to the design and that is the foundation of the rugs I make. I have made accent rugs, wall decorations, character designs, and much more. This is my favorite way to express my creativity while capitalizing on my skill.

Mindfulness

I believe the key to success is staying mindful of yourself and your surroundings. It is extremely important to understand how your own actions are affecting yourself and those surrounding you. I think it is easy for people to get too caught up in themselves without thinking about the repercussions of their own actions. This is why I try my best to give as much thought to my surroundings as I do to my own self. I hope that this leads to people trusting and counting on me to be their teammate.

Renewable Energy Techniques

I am a huge geek when it comes to renewable energy. In the long run I would love to tie my computer science skills to renewable energy to implement better energy practices in tech. I believe we only have a matter of time before the use of fossil fuels destroys the earth and it is our duty to fix this issue. Feel free to ask about my ideas for a more efficient future!
